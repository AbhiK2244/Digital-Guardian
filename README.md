# 🛡️ Digital Guardian

## Protect Your Digital Life

Digital Guardian is a comprehensive digital safety and cybersecurity awareness platform designed to help users learn, detect, and defend against modern online threats through interactive education and real-time protection tools.

---

## 🌍 Overview

The platform combines:

- Cybersecurity education
- Real-time scam detection
- Fake news verification
- Data breach monitoring
- Gamified learning experiences

into one unified digital safety ecosystem.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔍 Real-Time Detection
Detect:
- Phishing URLs
- Spam emails
- Suspicious links
- Malicious content

---

### 📰 AI-Powered News Verification
Verify local news credibility using AI-based analysis to help fight misinformation and fake news.

---

### 🔐 Breach Checker
Check whether your credentials have appeared in known data breaches.

---

### 🎮 Gamified Learning
- Earn points and badges
- Complete quizzes
- Unlock achievements
- Track your progress

---

### 📚 Educational Content

Learn about:
- Email Scams
- Fake News
- Social Media Safety
- Identity Theft
- Digital Footprints
- Data Protection
